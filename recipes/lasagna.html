lasagna

Description of Recipe:

To make a delicious lasagna:
(1)start by preparing the meat sauce. Sauté finely chopped onions and garlic in olive oil until translucent, then add ground beef and cook until browned. Stir in tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and a mix of Italian herbs such as basil, oregano, and thyme. Let it simmer for about 30 minutes.
(2)In a separate bowl, combine ricotta cheese, a beaten egg, and chopped fresh parsley.
(3)To assemble, spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish, followed by a layer of uncooked lasagna noodles. Top with a portion of the ricotta mixture, then sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Repeat these layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and a generous topping of mozzarella and Parmesan.
(4)Cover with aluminum foil and bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
(5)Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving.

Ingredients:

  • Olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 container (15 ounces) ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 12 lasagna noodles, uncooked
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Sauté finely chopped onions and minced garlic in olive oil until translucent.
  2. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil, oregano, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  4. Let the sauce simmer for about 30 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, a beaten egg, and chopped fresh parsley.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish.
  7. Add a layer of uncooked lasagna noodles over the sauce
  8. Spread a portion of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  9. Sprinkle with shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese.
  10. Repeat the layers: meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and Parmesan.
  11. Finish with a layer of meat sauce and a generous topping of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  12. Cover the assembled lasagna with aluminum foil.
  13. Bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes
  14. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  15. Allow the lasagna to rest for 10 minutes before serving.
